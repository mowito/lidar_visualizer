Instructions:


1: Create obstacles by selecting a tool and drawing on the canvas.

2: Select a LiDAR from the drop-down.

3. Change room length to reflect the real world.

4: Hit "Play" and move the cursor on the canvas to see how the LiDAR behaves. Use the mouse scroll wheel to change the direction.

5: Hit “Stop” to add more obstacles or to modify Range & Resolution values.

6: Hit “Reset” to clear the canvas..

Note:

1: In case the LiDAR has a range of values, the max value is used for visualization.

2: The tool is currently designed for 2D Visualization only.

3: If you want a new LiDAR to be added, please contact us!
